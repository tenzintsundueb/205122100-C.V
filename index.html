Analyzing Relationships in Game of Thrones With NetworkX and Gephi

This project delves into the features of the renowned television series, Game of Thrones. The captivating plotlines of the show present an ideal opportunity for utilizing network analysis and graph visualisation tools, two powerful and dynamic tools that will be utilized throughout our analysis.

APPROACH

Our approach includes visual analysis of networks through graphs to measure relationship strength, various houses(communities) in the Book and information flow between characters, to deepen our understanding of the properties of social network analysis and graph theory.

ANALYSIS
  • Character with most interaction(Degree Centrality)
  • Most Influential character(Betweenness Centrality)
  • Spread of Information between charcter( Closeness Centrality )
  • Identify the different Houses (Community Detection)
